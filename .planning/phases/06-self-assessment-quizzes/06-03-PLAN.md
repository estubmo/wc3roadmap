<objective>
Add a `sourceMap` slice to the graph store so both the panel label (D-14) and the graph-node visual (D-14) can read a node's mastery `source` (`quiz`/`manual`/`auto`) WITHOUT leaking the source into the `GraphDisplayNode` projection (ADR 002/005). Extend `ProgressProvider` to hydrate `sourceMap` from server progress records alongside `masteryMap`.

Purpose: The existing hydration only builds a `Record<string, MasteryState>` and discards `source`. Without a `sourceMap`, the quiz-mastered distinctions (panel + canvas) cannot render after a pass. This slice is the propagation seam Pitfall 4 (RESEARCH) warns about.
Output: `sourceMap` + `setSource` + `initSourceMap` on the store, and hydration wiring in `ProgressProvider`.
</objective>
