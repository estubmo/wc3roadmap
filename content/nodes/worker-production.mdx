# Continuous Worker Production

**Continuous worker production** is keeping your primary town building training workers without gaps
for the whole early game. It is the single highest-return macro habit because every idle production
second is income you never recover.

## Why It Matters

Workers compound: a Peasant, Peon, Acolyte, or Wisp made ten seconds earlier gathers ten seconds more
resources for the rest of its life. A single missed worker in the opening is worth far more than its
gold cost by the mid-game. Most economic deficits at intermediate level are not from bad decisions —
they are from small, unnoticed gaps in the worker queue while attention was elsewhere.

## How to Apply

For your next ten games, use the same drill from the opening node set: watch only your town building's
production queue for the first three minutes and never let it sit empty. Ignore the minimap and your
opponent. Once the queue never empties without you thinking about it, the habit has become automatic and
you can spend that freed attention on the rest of the game.
