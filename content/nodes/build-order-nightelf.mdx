# Night Elf Opening Build Order

The **standard Night Elf opening** sequences continuous Wisp production out of the Tree of Life, an Ancient
of War placed as early as gold allows, and a first Archer queued the moment the Ancient completes its
growth. Night Elf's opener is unique among the four races because a large chunk of the timeline is a fixed
building-growth timer rather than pure production efficiency.

## Why It Matters

Because the Ancient of War's growth time cannot be shortened, the skill in Night Elf's opener is in
everything around that fixed timer: an uninterrupted Wisp queue before the Ancient is placed, and
productive use of the growth window (scouting, pre-positioning for creeping) rather than idle waiting.
Players who treat the growth timer as "downtime" consistently arrive at their first combat window later
and less prepared than players who use it deliberately.

## How to Apply

Target: your **first Archer should complete before the 2:20 mark**. In your next ten games, place the
Ancient of War as early as your Wisp economy allows, then use the growth window specifically to scout your
opponent's base or begin your Hero's initial move — never leave that window idle. If your Archer is
consistently late, check whether the Ancient placement itself was delayed, since that is almost always the
single biggest lever on this timing. Mastery advances automatically once an uploaded replay clears the
threshold.
