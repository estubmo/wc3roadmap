# Undead Opening Build Order

The **standard Undead opening** sequences continuous Acolyte production out of the Necropolis, timely
Crypt placement, and a first Ghoul queued as soon as the Crypt completes. Because Acolytes double as
builders and resource gatherers, Undead's opener has more room for a silent, invisible mistake than any
other race.

## Why It Matters

A delayed Crypt or a misassigned Acolyte does not look like a mistake in the moment — production keeps
happening, gold keeps coming in — but it quietly pushes your first combat unit later than it should be.
Undead's map presence in the early game (creeping with Ghouls, contesting neutral buildings) depends
entirely on that first Ghoul arriving on schedule.

## How to Apply

Target: your **first Ghoul should complete before the 2:10 mark**. For your next ten games, explicitly
narrate your Acolyte assignments out loud or in your head as you make them — "this one builds Crypt, this
one gathers gold" — until the sequence feels automatic rather than deliberate. If your first Ghoul is
consistently late, check the replay for the exact moment the Crypt was placed; that single timestamp
usually explains the whole gap. Mastery advances automatically once an uploaded replay clears the
threshold.
