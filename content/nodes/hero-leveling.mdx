# Hero Leveling

**Hero leveling** is the discipline of maximising your Hero's experience gain in the opening minutes to reach
key power spikes ahead of your opponent. A Hero at level 5 fights dramatically differently from one at level 3.
Reaching those thresholds first creates a consistent early-game advantage.

## Why It Matters

Hero levels are multiplicative — a higher-level Hero wins fights that a lower-level Hero loses, which
means more gold, more experience, and more map control. Getting a level ahead in the early game
often snowballs through the mid-game if sustained.

## How to Apply

After each game, check the replay and note what level your Hero reached at the 3-minute, 5-minute,
and 8-minute marks. Compare against community benchmarks for your race. If you are below average,
identify which camps or creep waves you skipped or delayed. The fix is almost always an inefficiency
in your creep route or a moment spent idle rather than farming experience.
