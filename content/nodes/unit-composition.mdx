# Unit Composition

**Unit composition** is choosing which units to build so their damage and armor types answer what your
opponent is fielding, instead of producing the same army every game regardless of the matchup.

## Why It Matters

Warcraft III combat is decided as much by type matchups as by numbers. A smaller army with the right
damage type against the enemy's armor routinely beats a larger mismatched one. The skill is not
memorising every counter — it is reading the opponent's army as a recognised situation and reaching for
the trained response, which is only possible if you scouted what they have.

## How to Apply

Pause a replay the moment the enemy army is visible and, before un-pausing, name their composition and
the one adjustment you would make to counter it (add casters, switch to piercing, get dispel). Then watch
what happens. Over many reps you build a library of army-to-answer patterns, so in a live game the right
composition comes to mind without deliberating from scratch.
