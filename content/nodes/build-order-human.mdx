# Human Opening Build Order

The **standard Human opening** is a fixed, uninterrupted sequence: continuous Peasant production out of
Town Hall, a Barracks placed as soon as gold allows without stalling worker output, and a first Footman
queued the moment the Barracks completes. This node's mastery target is a real timing benchmark, not a
vague "expand quickly" — it is checked directly against your replay data.

## Why It Matters

Every second the Town Hall sits idle between Peasants is economy you never recover. Human's early game has
no slack for hesitation: the Barracks placement and first Footman timing set the pace for your whole
opening, and a late Footman means a late scout, a late second Barracks, and a late tech decision. Because
the sequence is short and fully repeatable, it is one of the fastest mechanics in the game to drill to
consistency.

## How to Apply

Target: your **first Footman should complete before the 2:00 mark**. In your next ten games, watch only
your Peasant queue and your Barracks placement — ignore the minimap and your opponent entirely for the
first two minutes. If your Footman consistently arrives after 2:00, the leak is almost always a gap in
worker production, not a slow Barracks placement. Once this replay is uploaded, mastery is detected
automatically the moment your parsed build order clears the 2:00 threshold — no self-report needed.
