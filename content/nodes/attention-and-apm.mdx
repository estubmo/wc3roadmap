# Attention and APM

**Attention and APM** is treating your attention as a limited budget and spending it on a deliberate
cycle, rather than chasing a high actions-per-minute number for its own sake. Effective APM is meaningful
actions spread across the whole game, not fast clicking on one spot.

## Why It Matters

Working memory is small: you physically cannot track your army, your economy, and the map at the same
instant. Players who fixate on a fight stop making workers and miss harassment; players who only macro get
caught out of position. The difference at higher levels is rarely raw speed — it is a disciplined rotation
of attention so nothing is neglected for long, made affordable by clean hotkeys.

## How to Apply

Drill a fixed scan cycle: act on the current fight, tap your town hotkey to check production, glance at the
minimap, repeat. Set control groups so each of those is a single keypress. For a few games, exaggerate the
cycle even when it feels slow — you are training the habit of never going more than a few seconds blind to
any one area. Speed comes later; the rotation is the skill.
