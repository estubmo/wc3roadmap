# Creep Routing

**Creep routing** is the planned sequence in which you clear neutral camps to maximise gold, experience,
and Hero levels in the opening minutes. A consistent route produces a predictable economic curve, making
it easier to spot when your opponent is disrupting your tempo.

## Why It Matters

Different camp sequences yield different XP distributions between your Hero and units. An optimised
route levels your Hero faster and reaches key power thresholds (level 3, level 5) at the most useful
game moments. Ad-hoc creeping — going to whichever camp seems closest — sacrifices this predictability.

## How to Apply

Before your next session, choose **one standard creep route** for your race and stick to it for five games.
Most community resources list tier-one routes by race and matchup. Focus on executing your chosen route
at the same order each time — not on optimising it yet. Consistency comes first; optimisation comes after
the route is internalised. Track whether you reach level 3 on your Hero at the same timing each game.
