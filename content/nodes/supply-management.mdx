# Supply Management

**Supply management** is the practice of maintaining enough food capacity to produce units without interruption.
Running into the supply cap — even briefly — halts unit production and hands your opponent a tempo advantage.

## Why It Matters

Every second your production is paused due to supply costs you a unit. Over the course of a game, repeated supply stalls compound into a significant army-size deficit.
Consistent supply management means your army grows steadily while your opponent's stalls at critical moments.

## How to Apply

Set a mental rule: start building your next supply structure (Farm, Burrow, Altar, etc.) when you reach **70% of your current cap**.
In your next five games, track how many times your gold sits above 500 with supply at cap. Each instance is a free unit you did not build.
Focus first on never supply-stalling during a fight — that is the highest-impact moment to correct.
