# Micro: Focus Fire

**Focus fire** is the practice of directing your units to attack one target at a time rather than
letting the game's auto-attack AI split damage across multiple enemies. Auto-attack spreads damage
evenly; focus fire kills units sequentially, reducing the opponent's total damage output faster.

## Why It Matters

A dead unit deals zero damage. Distributing damage evenly across five enemy units means all five
remain alive and fighting for longer. Killing them one at a time eliminates each unit's contribution
immediately. The math consistently favours focus fire — often deciding close fights.

## How to Apply

In your next fight, identify the most dangerous unit in your opponent's army (usually: the highest-damage
unit, a caster with a key ability, or the lowest-HP unit if kills are close). Before the fight starts,
right-click that target with all your army. After it dies, immediately pick the next target. This is
not about speed — it is about intentionality. Even slow, deliberate focus fire outperforms automatic
target-splitting.
