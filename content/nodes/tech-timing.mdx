# Tech Timing

**Tech timing** is the deliberate decision of when to invest in Tier 2 and Tier 3 upgrades relative
to your current army strength, expansion status, and your opponent's technology progress. Teching too
early delays your army; teching too late means fighting a more advanced opponent with an outdated composition.

## Why It Matters

Tier upgrades gate powerful units and abilities. Reaching Tier 2 one minute ahead of your opponent means
your Tier-2 units fight their Tier-1 units — a composition mismatch that wins decisive engagements.
Reaching Tier 2 one minute behind means the reverse. The leverage of timing is asymmetric.

## How to Apply

Before each game, commit to a tech path based on the matchup and write it down: "Tier 2 at approximately
X minutes, then Y upgrade or unit immediately after." At the end of the game, check your replay to see
whether your tech reached completion at the intended moment and whether it arrived in time to be used
in the decisive fight. Adjust the timing target based on what you observe, not on a generic rule.
