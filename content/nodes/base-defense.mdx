# Base Defense

**Base defense** is the coordinated response to an attack on your main base or expansion, using
a combination of defensive structures, retreating units, and Hero abilities to minimise damage and
buy time. Effective base defense preserves your economy and forces your opponent to over-commit
or retreat.

## Why It Matters

A lost base is usually a lost game. But many base-defense failures are avoidable — they happen
because the defending player arrives late, engages in the wrong order (attacking units before
towers are firing), or does not use the terrain advantage that static defences provide.
Scouting makes defense reactive rather than passive; positioning shapes the defensive engagement
the same way it shapes any fight.

## How to Apply

After your next base defense replay, answer three questions: (1) Did you have warning before the
attack arrived — and if not, how did your opponent avoid your scouting? (2) Did your defensive
structures deal damage before your units engaged — if not, your units arrived before the attackers
reached your towers. (3) Did you retreat wounded units before they died, or did you let them
die in melee? Each of these is a discrete, improvable behaviour with a clear correct action.
