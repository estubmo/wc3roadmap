# Resource Banking

**Resource banking** is the pattern of holding more gold and lumber than your current production
and technology queue can absorb. Idle resources are lost tempo — every second gold sits unspent
above your production capacity is a unit or upgrade you declined to build.

## Why It Matters

WC3 resources do not roll over strategically — a 600-gold buffer during a fight does not make you
stronger during that fight. The gold only helps if it was spent on units before the fight. Consistent
resource banking means your army is smaller than your income warrants, which compounds into an army
deficit over time.

## How to Apply

Open a recent replay and watch your gold at 30-second intervals. Identify the three highest banking
moments. For each, ask: "What should I have spent this on?" The answer is almost always: an additional
unit, an upgrade, or an expansion. Once you identify the correct spend for each banking moment, add
a trigger in your next live game — when gold exceeds 300 with production idle, immediately queue
something. The exact unit matters less than breaking the banking habit.
