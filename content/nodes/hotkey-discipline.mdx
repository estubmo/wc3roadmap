# Hotkey Discipline

**Hotkey discipline** is the commitment to using keyboard shortcuts for every repeatable action in the game.
Selecting buildings, control groups, abilities, and items via hotkey frees up mouse bandwidth for the
positioning decisions that actually win fights.

## Why It Matters

Every mouse click on a UI element is a click not spent on the battlefield. High-APM WC3 players spend
their mouse time on unit positioning and ability targeting — not clicking barracks buttons. Hotkeys
collapse production cycles into muscle memory, freeing cognitive capacity for strategic decisions.

## How to Apply

Pick one action you currently click with the mouse — your Town Hall, your primary barracks, or your
first control group. For your next ten games, force yourself to use only the hotkey for that action.
Expect it to feel slower at first; that is normal. Speed returns after approximately 3–5 sessions
of deliberate practice. Once one action is automatic, add another.
